Refund & Return Policy

At Brewverse, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your coffee order, we're here to help.


Returns

We understand that sometimes things don't go as expected. If you wish to return a product, please review the following guidelines:

  • Eligibility for Return: Due to the perishable nature of our coffee drinks, we are unable to accept returns once the product has been opened or consumed.
  • Damaged or Incorrect Orders:If your coffee drink arrives damaged, incorrect, or you believe there was an error with your order, please contact us within 7 days of receiving it. We will offer a full refund or replacement at no additional cost.
  • Exclusions: Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns on opened, consumed, or time-sensitive products like iced or cold brew drinks.

Refunds

  • How to Request a Refund: If you would like a refund due to a damaged, defective, or incorrect order, please reach out to our customer service team at contactBrewverse@gmail.com with your order number, a brief description of the issue, and a photo if applicable.
  • Processing Time: Once your refund is approved, it will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • Shipping Costs: If your return is due to a mistake on our part (e.g., damaged or incorrect items), we will cover the cost of return shipping. If you're returning a non-defective product, return shipping will be at your expense.

Exchanges

If you're dissatisfied with your coffee drink (e.g., wrong flavor or style), we'll do our best to accommodate an exchange within 7 days of delivery. Contact us to discuss the available options for an exchange or a different drink.
